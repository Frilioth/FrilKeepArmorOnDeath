# Keep Armor On Death

A simple mod for **7 Days to Die (V2.6)** that keeps your armor visible on your player model during the death animation. No more dying naked.

## What It Does

When you die in vanilla, the game strips your equipment visually before the ragdoll plays. This mod blocks that visual update while you're dead — your character keeps wearing whatever armor they had on. Items still drop normally into your death bag.

After 3 seconds (once the ragdoll is active), the equipment data is cleared properly to prevent duplication on respawn.

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Frilioth/FrilKeepArmorOnDeath/releases)
2. Extract the `FrilKeepArmorOnDeath` folder into your `Mods` directory
3. Launch the game with **EAC disabled**

Requires [0_TFP_Harmony](https://7daystodiemods.com/harmony-mod/).

## Compatibility

- Works in single player and multiplayer
- Works alongside other mods
- No XML changes, no config needed
